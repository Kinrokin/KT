# KT Constitutional Program Charter

KT exists to execute under law, evolve under law, prove its own improvement under law, and remain externally legible under adversarial review.

## Permanent Ends

- stronger truth
- stronger boundaries
- stronger lawful evolution
- stronger external legibility

## Immediate Order Of Execution

1. settle authority on the pinned head
2. freeze sacred canonical surfaces
3. enforce six-zone law across readiness and promotion
4. ratify lab outputs only through promotion law
5. extend external verifier and capability-atlas surfaces only after the first four hold

## Constitutional Rules

- current posture may only come from the active current-head truth source
- canonical, lab, archive, commercial, generated runtime truth, and quarantined are distinct legal zones
- no lab output may enter canonical without proof, rollback, and risk accounting
- frozen canonical surfaces require amendment scope and OP multisig
- documentary, historical, and quarantined surfaces must never be read as active posture
