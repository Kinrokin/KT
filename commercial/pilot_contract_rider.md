# Bounded Pilot Contract Rider

Pilot scope is limited to KT verifier, evidence-pack, and claim-compiler review.

The pilot does not represent external audit acceptance, independent certification, unrestricted production readiness, S-tier status, beyond-SOTA status, or 7B amplification proof.

The pilot participant may provide feedback. Feedback becomes independent attestation only if it satisfies the independent attestation schema and reviewer independence requirements.
