# Operating Doctrine

Do not train harder. Train more causally.
Do not make prompts harder. Make pressure more diagnostic.
Do not chase metrics. Pair every metric against its failure mode.
Do not claim learning. Prove scar conversion.
Do not claim utility. Prove utility under constraint.
Do not train every failure. Classify every failure.
Do not treat restraint as inaction. Formal restraint is a repair act.

No crucible enters Academy pressure unless it declares diagnostic discriminators and repair surface mapping.
