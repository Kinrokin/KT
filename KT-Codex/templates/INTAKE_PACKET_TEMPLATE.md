## Purpose (plain-English)
The intake packet freezes scope and defines acceptance before any evaluation runs. It prevents scope drift from turning into unverifiable claims. [SRC:REG:NIST-AI-RMF-1.0]

## Fields
- Customer name and contacts.
- System identifier (commit/version).
- Intended use and risk context.
- Data boundaries (what may be processed, stored, and delivered).
- Prohibited content categories for deliverables.
- Acceptance artifacts list (what constitutes PASS). [SRC:NEEDS_VERIFICATION]
